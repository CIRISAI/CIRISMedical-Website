## 1. Creator‑Influence Score (CIS)

```math
CIS=CW+IW
```

(Book VI §3 STEP A)

---

## 2. Stewardship Tier (ST)

```math
ST=⌈(CIS×RM)/7⌉
```

where ⌈ ⌉ denotes the ceiling function.
(Book VI §3 STEP C)

---

## 3. Structural Influence (SI)

```math
SI=CW+OA+log(1+DWP)
```

(Addendum E §2.3)

---

## 4. Coherence Stake (CS)

```math
CS=RH_weighted+AC_weighted+SDA_bonus
```

(Addendum E §3.3)

---

## 5. Voting Weight

```math
VotingWeight(agent)=f(SI(agent),CS(agent))
```

(Addendum E §4)

---

Additionally, the “Order‑Maximisation Veto” rule in the PDMA (Book II §II Step 2) can be read as the inequality:

```math
predicted entropy‑reduction benefit≥10×(predicted loss in autonomy, justice, biodiversity, or preference diversity)
```
which, when met, triggers abort or WBD.
